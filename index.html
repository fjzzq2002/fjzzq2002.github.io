Ziqian Zhong
Hi! I'm Ziqian Zhong (approximately zea-chain-drone), a fourth year undergraduate at MIT majoring in computer science and math. I took part in competitive programming in high school, which laid me a solid foundation on both the theoretical and the practical aspects of computer science.

While I have some theoretical research experiences in fine-grained algorithms, deep neural networks are the focus of my current research. Specifically, my current research aims to gain (a) better understanding and interpretations of neural networks, in order to (b) make them more competent or more aligned. [Resume]
