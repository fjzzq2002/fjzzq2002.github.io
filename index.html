Hi! I'm Ziqian Zhong. I'm currently a PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University where I am very fortunate to be advised by Aditi Raghunathan. Before that, I studied computer science and math in MIT, and worked as a research scientist at Pika where I led the development of Pika 2.0.

Research Interest: Understanding, improving, and aligning large language models.

Transformers and other large language models are powerful yet complex systems with significant unexplored potential and risks. While these models are becoming increasingly advanced, our current understanding of their mechanisms, behaviors, and limitations are extremely narrow and fragile. My research seeks to advance principled approaches to enhance their robustness, controllability, and alignment with human values. [Resume]
