Hi! I'm Ziqian Zhong (zea-chain-drone), a third year undergraduate at MIT majoring in computer science and math. I took part in competitive programming in high school, which laid me a solid foundation on both the theoretical and the practical aspects of computer science.

While I have some theoretical research experiences in fine-grained algorithms, my current research interest lies in interpreting and understanding deep neural networks.
